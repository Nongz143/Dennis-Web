bulbasaur Details:

Bulbasaur is a small, quadrupedal Pokémon that is primarily blue-green in color. It has a dark green bulb on its back, which grows as it ages. It is known for its gentle nature and its strong connection to nature.

Charmander Details:

Charmander is a small, bipedal lizard-like Pokémon with a yellow-orange body. It has a flame at the tip of its tail, which burns brightly when it is healthy and can flicker or burn out if it is weak or sick. It evolves into Charmelon.
